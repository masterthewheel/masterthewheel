1. Who We Are

Master the Wheel (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides automatic driving lessons in East London. Our contact address is Dagenham, RM9, East London. Email: info@masterthewheel.co.uk. Phone: 07552 522479.

2. Information We Collect

When you contact us or submit a booking form, we may collect: your name, phone number, email address, and your message or course enquiry. We do not collect payment details directly through this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiry or confirm your lesson booking
  • To send you lesson reminders or follow-ups if you have booked
  • To improve our services

We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interest (responding to your enquiry) and contract performance (fulfilling a booked lesson).

5. Data Retention

We retain your contact data only as long as necessary to provide the requested service. Enquiry data is typically deleted within 12 months if no booking is made.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@masterthewheel.co.uk.
